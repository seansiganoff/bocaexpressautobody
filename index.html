ABOUT US

Our team has more then 20 years experience in the auto body and paint industry. Because of our quality work and reasonable prices, we have been serving Boca Raton for over 10 years.
We are one of few body shops in Boca Raton that received an I-CAR and GOLD CLASS CERTIFICATION. Thats because we are passionate about our work, and we adhere to the industries best practices.

We use top quality industry products on all our repairs, and thats why we guarantee our work to last for at least 5 years.
Our goal is to provide the best customer service and deliver quality work at a reasonable price.

OUR SERVICES:

Body:

Dents, rust, holes, plastic bumper dents/cracks/holes, parts replacement, rim repair and painting, Fiberglass repair, classic restoration, welding.

Paint:

Paint jobs, scratches, paint correction (3 stages), touch-up, chips, oxidation, ceramic coating, clay Bar.

Other:

Plasti-dip (spray on pealable paint coating), detailing, headliners, custom seats, sound systems, windshields, decals.
